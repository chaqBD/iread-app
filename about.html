Who We Are

About iRead

Three doctoral researchers. One shared conviction: in the age of AI, those who actually read will lead.

Our Mission

A community built on intellectual rigour

To cultivate a UK-wide community of doctoral researchers who engage rigorously with management literature — resisting intellectual shortcuts, championing deep reading, and generating critical dialogue that advances theory and practice.

Three doctoral researchers at the University of Huddersfield — specialising in AI Supply Chain Analytics, Leadership, and Algorithmic Work — founded iRead because they believe that in an era where AI summarises everything, the researchers who actually read will be the ones who discover what AI cannot.
